# Horsera — Go-To-Market Context

_Last updated: 2026-03-13_

## Current Phase: MVP Validation

We are pre-launch, building toward a barn-based user testing cohort before 2026-03-30.

## Near-Term GTM (Pre-Launch)

### Target: Barn Testing Cohort
- Small group of riders at Rossella's barn
- Goal: validate core loop (upload video → get biomechanics analysis → Cadence feedback → "I see myself improving")
- Distribution: direct link to horsera.app (PWA), no App Store needed
- Feedback focus: accuracy of biomechanics, usefulness of Cadence, emotional resonance

## Investor Track

- YC application in progress
- Key narrative: longitudinal system of record as moat, $50B+ market with no full-loop solution
- Traction milestone needed: user testing results + early retention signal

## Channel Strategy (Planned)

### Community-Led
- Equestrian communities are tight-knit and trust peer recommendations
- Target: riding clubs, barn networks, online equestrian communities (Facebook groups, Reddit r/Equestrian, equestrian Instagram)
- Strategy: give serious amateur riders early access, let results speak

### Content & Social
- Video content showing real biomechanics analysis (before/after, "aha moment" clips)
- Rider testimonials focused on the emotional insight moment
- Platform focus: Instagram (equestrian community is highly visual), TikTok (younger riders), YouTube (longer-form education)

### Trainer Channel (V2)
- Trainers as distribution: if Horsera is valuable to their students, trainers will recommend it
- Consider trainer early-access program with free or reduced pricing

## Messaging Hierarchy

1. **Primary hook:** "See yourself improving — for the first time"
2. **Proof:** AI biomechanics analysis that shows exactly what's happening in your body as you ride
3. **Stickiness:** Your riding history compounds. The longer you use it, the smarter it gets about you.
4. **Aspiration:** Built for riders who are serious about getting better

## Pricing Strategy

Freemium or low-friction trial for MVP validation. Paid tiers introduced post-validation:
- Riders: $8–15/mo
- Trainers: $20–40/mo
- Barns: $40–75/mo
